Projects / Overview

Coptic Scriptorium has been supported by a series of funded projects:

  • Coptic Scriptorium – Digitizing a Corpus for Interdisciplinary Research in Ancient Egyptian (NEH Preservation and Access grant) and A Corpus, Tools, and Methods for Corpus Linguistics and Computational Historical Research in Ancient Egypt (ODH Start-Up grant)
  • Kellia – Koptische/Coptic Electronic Language and Literature International Alliance
  • Digital Humanities Advancement Grant – A Linked Digital Environment for Coptic Studies: Integrating Heterogeneous Data with Machine Learning and Natural Language Processing
