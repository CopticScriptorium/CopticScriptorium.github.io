About this Add-on:

The digital transcription was adapted from various other electronic versions. This database is a tool to identify Sahidic Coptic biblical fragments, especially those of the dispersed White Monastery library. In some cases, the St Shenouda Coptic CD transcriptions were converted, but in all cases the text has been corrected and some are newly digitized (e.g., Psalms [M.Schulz], John[H.Förster etal.], Acts [Siegfried Richter], Revelation [Chr.Askeland]). No doubt there are still errors. The database currently reflects the testimony of the following manuscripts or scholarly editions, with various emendations:

BL Or 5000 (Ps), M569 (Mat), PPalau Ribes 181–183 (Mk, Lk, John), BL Or 7594 (Acts), Ch.Beat. Coptic A (Paul.Ep.), M572 (Cath.Epist.), BL Or 6803 (Rev)

This is the beta version.
